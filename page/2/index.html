According to Masonic historian Arturo de Hoyos, the term “Jahbulon” was first used in the 18th century in early French versions of the Royal Arch degree, and it was not until the 19th century that it was officially adopted. An allegory of Masonry is told in which Jabulon is identified as an explorer who lived during the time of Solomon and was credited with discovering the ruins of an ancient temple.
