    Tom Ford brought his Fall 2020 fashion show to Los Angeles this year. He's no stranger to the bright lights and big names of Hollywood, but he brought in three of the biggest names to walk the show â€” and sent them all out wearing completely sheer dresses. Kendall Jenner, Bella Hadid, and Gigi Hadid all stomped down the catwalk and every one of their showstopping dresses was skin-tight, see-through, and pretty perfect for the red carpet this weekend.

    Bella Hadid's crystal-covered dress featured a halter neckline with a vast swath of bare skin, shimmering gems, and two black velvet bows. Jennifer Lopez, who was sitting in the front row, was the first to get on her feet and applaud the collection, Vogue notes, and the dress seemed tailor-made for a superstar like her. Bella's sister, Gigi, was slightly more demure, though her lace dress was just as sheer, showing off a pair of black underwear. The silhouette was the only thing modest about it, with a high turtleneck, long sleeves, and a floor-skimming hem, but the open pattern of the lace left very little to the imagination.

    Jenner wore a mashup of the two, with a black lace dress that borrowed the same bow details as Bella's and the same long lines and black lace as Gigi's.

    Though these dresses are undoubtedly the most attention-grabbing looks, the trio of models other outfits in the presentation, too. Ford showed patchwork denim, bohemian mixed prints, and oversized knits, which the Hadids and Jenner also wore, though it'll be tough to forget these three looks when Fashion Week wraps up.
