    Rena Frazier is an American realtor and a TV personality. She is a realtor from Tampa Bay and also has been featured in Netflix's reality TV show called Selling Tampa alongside other realtors like Sharelle Rosado, Anne-Sophie Petit, and Colony Reeves. The show was a spin-off to Selling Sunset and unfortunately was poorly received. 

    Frazier besides real estate is also an attorney and she has an estimated net worth of $1.5 Million. The realtor is a mother of five kids. The latter four of her kids are shared with her husband, Anddrikk Frazier while Ariana came from her previous relationship with Adrian Mosley.

    Relationship Status: The Realtor Is A Married Woman

    Rena Frazier has found the love of her life as she is happily married to a man named Anddrikk Frazier. The two exchanged their wedding vows on May 31, 2003. 

    Rena & Anddrikk first crossed paths at the University of South Florida and that's where their love story started. The couple doesn't hesitate to show their love for one another through their social media.

    Frazier's husband, Anddrikk currently works as the President and CEO of Best Source Consulting. He has previously held the same position in another company called Integral Energy. He has also worked in companies like Trillium CNG, TECO Energy, and Tampa Electric. 

    Net Worth Of The Tampa Bay Star

    Rena Frazier through her successful career has gained an estimated net worth of $1.5 Million. Her work in the real estate industry can be credited to her wealth. 

    Frazier has found success dealing with properties in Tampa Bay. She, at the time of this writing, will soon open her brokerage company called Realty LV. 

    Frazier has also made some money in the past working on television. The realtor has been part of the Netflix show called Selling Tampa. 

    Unfortunately, the show didn't do well, and the streaming service has decided not to continue forward with the show for now. Besides all of this, she also owns an interior company called, Greycliff Interiors. 

    Mother Of Five Kids

    Rena Frazier when is not working is occupied by her five kids. Among her kids, four of them are daughters named, Ariana Mosley, Aja Frazier, Alivia Frazier, and Aryn Frazier. Her only son is called, Aero Jaiden Frazier. 

    Frazier's oldest child is daughter Ariana who came from her previous marriage to Adrian Mosley. Her daughter decided to make a career in a field completely different than that from hers. She is serving her nation after graduating from the US Air Force Academy.

    The realtor, Frazier's other kids are shared with her current husband. Her youngest child is her only son, Aero. He was born on June 10, 2022. She had always wanted a big family. People have mentioned that the realtor always wanted five kids but had doubts because of her age. Her oldest Ariana was also pregnant with her son when Aero was in the womb of Frazier. 

    About Frazier's Realtor Career

    Selling Tampa star, Frazier is a successful realtor in Tampa Bay. She worked for Allure Realty for a significant part of her career. Axios reports that she has started her company called Realty LV which remains to be open at the time of this writing. She will be working as a broker in her company.

    Frazier worked at Allure Realty for more than two years. She worked at the company as a Realtor/Broker associate from September 2019 till April 2022 as per her Linkedin page. She obtained her license to practice as a realtor in 2018 and only got her broker's license three years later in 2021.

    Rena Frazier Was Previously Married

    Rena Frazier had previously been in a married relationship before Anddrikk Frazier. She was the wife of a man named Adrian Mosley. Both of them have kept their marriage to themselves so, the information on their marriage date and how they met remains a mystery at the moment.

    Mosley also lives a quiet life away from the media, unlike his ex-wife. But we do know what he does for a living. He isn't a celebrity but rather is serving his nation through the US Air Force as a sergeant.

    Frazier's oldest daughter, Ariana has followed in her father, Mosley's footsteps as she is also in the US Air Force. He was present when his daughter graduated from the Air Force Academy. His ex-wife had even shared a picture on Twitter where he and Ariana salute each other. 

    Educational Background

    Rena Frazier has an educational background in law and engineering. She holds a degree in both of these fields. The Selling Tampa star studied law at the Stetson University College of Law and engineering at the University of South Florida. The latter one is where Meredith Yeomans got her journalism degree from.

    Frazier holds a Bachelor's degree in Civil and Environmental engineering. Her Linkedin page mentions that she studied at the University of South Florida from 1997 to 2001. And, she studied at Stetson from 2001 to 2004.

    The Selling Tampa star, Frazier's time at Stetson was more than just getting a degree as she has been honored with a few other achievements as well. Her Linkedin page mentioned that she was a member of the trial team which was nationally known. The realtor's participation in the National Trial competition has also seen her being honored with the George A. Speigelberg Award.

    Rena Frazier In Selling Tampa

    Frazier entered the showbiz industry after appearing in a reality show, Selling Tampa. The show was produced by Netflix and came out in 2021. The show was about an all-black female real estate agent who tries to become a big company in Tampa Bay. 

    Selling Tampa included other realtors like Sharelle Rosado, Anne-Sophie Petit, Colony Reeves, Juawana Colbert, Alexis Williams, and Tenille Moore. The first season had a total of eight episodes and unfortunately was received poorly by the audiences. This is why the show has been canceled at the time of this writing. Only time will tell if they will bring back the show.

    Is Also A Lawyer

    Frazier besides dealing with properties is also an advocate. She holds a lawyer's license and is a practicing lawyer too. She currently serves as the Principal Attorney at her own Rena Frazier Law. She has been doing this since April 2018, the same month Nathalie Kelly married Jordan Burrows.

    The Tampa Bay realtor, Frazier has previously worked as the attorney for FOWLER WHITE BOGGS. She was with the company for more than six years, starting in January 2004 to November 2010. 

    Frazier has accomplished a lot in the field of Law as she has done in real estate. Her Linkedin page mentioned that she was the only black woman to have been partnered at the National Law Firm at one point. She helps with business legal issues related to real estate as mentioned on her official website.
