    Dennis Hastert was born on January 2, 1942 in Aurora, IL and was the longest-serving Republican Speaker of the House of Representatives in US history. During his time in office, he was a strong advocate for fiscal responsibility and was a key figure in the passage of the Balanced Budget Act of 1997.
