    Who Is Dashaun Wesley? 

    Dashaun Wesley is a multitalented star, he is an actor, TV personality, host, and emcee. He was born on the 24th of August 1984 and is 36 years old as of June 2021. He was born in the USA and has American nationality along with African American ethnicity.
