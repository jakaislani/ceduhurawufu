    Bryce Horton is most famous for being the husband of Krista Horton, a parenting and lifestyle blogger.

    They have been married since 2013.

    Bryce and Krista have two sons together, Boston and Kampbell, and they have a daughter named Kollyns.

    Bryce first appeared on Instagram in February 2013.

    The pair have shared several posts about their family, travels, and fashion on Instagram, and Bryce has since gained hundreds of thousands of followers.

    What does Bryce do for a living?

    He worked there for three years before he decided to move to a new company.
