    Grant Milliron was a prominent figure in the recycling industry and a respected philanthropist in the Shelby community. 

    He died on July 26th, 2023, at the age of 88. 

    His death has left behind a deep impact on the recycling industry and the lives of many in the community. 

    The details surrounding his passing have not been revealed publicly. His legacy lives on through his positive impact on the community.

    Grant Milliron’s philanthropic efforts, entrepreneurial spirit, and commitment to the betterment of his community and the environment have touched the lives of many. 

    His contributions to recycling and community development have earned him admiration and respect from all who knew him. 

    He pioneered the recycling industry as the founder and owner of Milliron Recycling Company.

    He created jobs and economic growth for the region while paving the way for a more sustainable future.

    A Philanthropist’s journey

    Grant Milliron was born in Mansfield, Ohio 1935, and spent his entire life in Richland County. 

    At 18 years old, he founded Milliron Recycling Company, which would go on to thrive for 64 years before its sale to BlueScope Recycling and Materials, LLC in 2022. 

    Throughout his successful entrepreneurial journey, he remained dedicated to his community, consistently giving back and supporting local causes. 

    His generosity played a significant role in the construction of a new fire station in Shelby in 2016, marking his commitment and dedication to the community he called home.

    Grant Milliron’s story

    Grant Milliron was not only a successful entrepreneur, but he was also a devoted family man. 

    He was married to Mary Milliron for 67 years, and together they raised seven children, three sons, and four daughters. 

    Their enduring love and fruitful marriage stand as a testament to the values of family, community, and hard work that Grant held so close to his heart. 

    Milliron’s commitment to his family was unwavering, and it reflected his commitment to the broader community.

    A Lasting Impact on Shelby community

    Grant Milliron’s contributions to the Shelby community will undoubtedly leave a lasting impact. 

    As a leader in the recycling industry and a community philanthropist, he embodied the values of hard work, dedication, and generosity that were fundamental to his success. 

    His contribution to the construction of the new fire station was just one example of how his dedication to Shelby transcended his business ventures. 

    He will be remembered for his philanthropy and his unwavering commitment to the community he loved.

    Remembering Grant Milliron

    Grant Milliron’s unexpected passing has profoundly impacted the Shelby community. 

    His contributions to the region’s economic growth, community development, and sustainability will remain forever ingrained in the community’s history. 

    As Shelby’s friends, family, and residents mourn the loss of this remarkable individual, they will also celebrate his life’s accomplishments. 

    Grant Milliron will be remembered for his dedication to philanthropy and entrepreneurial spirit. 

    His commitment to the betterment of his community and the environment was commendable.
